About Me

Hi! I’m Shreyas. Some things you should probably know about me:

  • I’m a senior at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology.
  • I’m a co-captain of TJHSST Science Olympiad. Science Olympiad is a national science competition. I’ve participated in Science Olympiad since eighth grade and gone to nationals with TJ twice.
  • I’m also a founder and lead maintainer of Duosmium Results, a popular Science Olympiad results archive and publisher.
  • I am a Lead Student Systems Administrator at TJ’s Computer Systems Lab. Some of my work as a Sysadmin has included solving mail security issues and conducting the largest overhaul of the school’s DNS in over a decade.
  • I’m also a clarinetist in TJ’s band program. I participated in the curricular ensembles for three years and the marching band for four.
  • I translate a little Latin from time to time.
  • When I’m not doing any of this, I’m probably reading Reddit/Wikipedia/the Washington Post or playing Civilization.
